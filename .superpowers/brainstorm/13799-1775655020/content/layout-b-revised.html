B1) Actions as Bottom Bar

Actions live at the very bottom of the view, always below whatever tab content you're looking at. Primary action (Update Status) is a full-width highlighted button. Secondary actions (Comment, Msg Rep, Msg Cust) are a row below it. Prev/Next below that. Actions are always reachable by scrolling down — they follow the content.

B2) Actions Between Header & Tabs

Status selector and action buttons sit right below the ticket header, above the tabs. You see the ticket, immediately see what you can do, then scroll down into context tabs. Status change is a single tap on the status bar. Actions are always visible without scrolling on most phones.

B3) Sticky Action Footer

Actions are fixed to the bottom of the screen (above the bottom nav). They never scroll away — always one thumb-tap away no matter where you are in the content. Header sticks to top, tabs + content scroll in the middle, action bar fixed at bottom. Like a toolbar that's always there.
