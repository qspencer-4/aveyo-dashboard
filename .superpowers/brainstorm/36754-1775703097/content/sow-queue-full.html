Design Notes

  • Chips scroll horizontally — 10 views, each shows count
  • Active chip has white border, others dimmed
  • Left border color: blue (good), white (neutral), red (rejections/overdue)
  • Progress bar: blue when on track, red when low completion + old
  • Timer colors: blue (<5d), white (5-10d), red (>10d)
  • Rows are not tappable yet — detail view comes later
