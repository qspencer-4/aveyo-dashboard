A) Inline Expand

Tapping an action expands a compose area right below the buttons, pushing the tabs down. You type, submit, and it collapses back. Everything stays on one page — no overlays, no navigation. Simple and predictable.

B) Bottom Sheet

A panel slides up from the bottom, dimming the ticket view behind it. Focused compose experience — nothing else competes for attention. Dismiss by tapping X or swiping down. Feels native on iPhone.

C) Scroll to Compose

Tapping an action auto-scrolls to a compose section at the bottom of the page. Consistent with how the existing project detail view already works. Familiar pattern, but you lose sight of the ticket header.
